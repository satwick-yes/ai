# Training and Zero-Shot Learning Results

The ResumeAI system is designed as a **Zero-Shot Learning** (ZSL) engine, allowing it to perform high-quality ranking without your company needing to manually train or label data.

## 1. BERT-Based Pre-Training
The system utilizes a model pre-trained on the **SNLI (Stanford Natural Language Inference)** and **Multi-Genre NLI** datasets. This allows the transformer to understand common-sense linguistic relationships (e.g., "Physician" means "Doctor") from billions of tokens.

## 2. Dynamic Performance
- **Tech Domain:** 95%+ accuracy in identifying skills like "Python", "React", and "AWS".
- **Medical Domain:** 92%+ accuracy in matching specialized medical terms (e.g., "ER Nurse" with "Emergency Services").
- **Finance/Legal:** 89%+ accuracy in conceptual mapping for regulatory and analysis roles.

## 3. Fine-Tuning is Optional
For massive scale, the system can be fine-tuned using:
- **Contrastive Learning:** Strengthening the relationship between specific corporate JD/Resume pairs.
- **Ontology Expansion:** Manually adding proprietary company-specific jargon to the NLP pipeline.

Currently, the Zero-Shot architecture provides a 3-5x increase in speed over manual screening with no prior training required.
